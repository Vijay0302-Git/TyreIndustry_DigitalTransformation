7Transformation Pattern Analysis▾
  • First major digital initiative observed: Implementation of a global SAP ERP foundation mentioned in 2016 for financial and commercial discipline.
  • Most frequently used tools: Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) for multi-location energy and speed control; SAP ERP as the core enterprise software.
  • Evidence of upgrades/replacements: The 2021 report details a ₹450 Cr capital expenditure for the "Modernization and Technology Upgradation" of existing equipment in Rajasthan and Bhuj; the 2024 report highlights the transition to i-Ram2.0 technology for mixing operations.
  • Any repeated investments without clear benefits: Continuous, massive annual Capex in vertical integration (Carbon Black and Mould plants) to hedge supply chain risks; while objectives are clear (hedging), specific standalone financial returns on these individual units are not isolated from total tire revenue.
  • Areas with strongest measurable improvement: Dispatch (15% packing efficiency gain via automatic packers in 2021); Manufacturing (100% accuracy in carbon loading via RFID in 2023); Shop Floor (elimination of hard copies in 2025 via Mini PCs).
  • Areas with no visible improvement: Outbound Logistics Cost as a % of sales; despite digital efforts and AEO Tier III status, costs peaked at 11.5% in FY23 due to external "unprecedented ocean freight rates" and disruptions.
